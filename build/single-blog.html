Blue inspired content to kick off the new year

Graphic Design
Blue is the official Pantone color for 2017 so to celebrate we invited our community members to share their favorite content across the entire ecosystem based on the color blue.
Symbolizing nature and calmness, what better way to be inspired in the new year that through the “new beginning” of the color green!
